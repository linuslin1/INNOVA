Problem¶

In this project, we are building a portfolio on future prediction of stock market in one year time. We are going to compare 5 companies in each of the following three industries: IT, Retail and Bank. Then finding out the best stock in each industry based on the data provided and our analysis. Well put them into a portfolio that only shows stocks with grander prospects. Our analysis will be based on the daily stock price trend of each picked individual stock from 2008 to 2014.

The reason we are doing this project is we are trying to apply what we have learned from statistics for dealing with the quantive finance. Therefore, we can help the customer to make profit from buy/sell stocks. In other words, we are trying to construct and predict the stock price to help customer making profits from purchasing stocks.

Data¶

We get our data from Yahoo Finance, which has provided daily stock market price information in a large spreadsheet on its website. These data is in a format of API data. In addition, we also acquire the LIBOR rate from the official website of Federal Reserve. They will be used to quantify the stock market trend in each specific field. We use the internet interactive facilities of Python, Bash, and R to get different datasets, highlight the importance, graph the trend and organize them into dataframe portfolio.

Main challenge of this project¶

Since we analysis 3 different area of stock, and each area contains 5 differetnt type of stock, it would be really unefficent to repeat all of the codes for 15 times. Therefore, the biggest challenge for us is to come up with some functions that can make it simple and efficent.

Also, the financial engineering are difficult for us (Statistics undergraduate). We have to do lots of research in different resources to learn the Quantive finance theory, and how to apply it through R. These courses are mainly learned from Master / Phd level of financial engineering.

Presentation of our Finding¶

From now on, we can imagine the customer have 15 favourite stocks chosed from 3 different industry. We, as consultant, help him to pick the 3 best stocks to construct the profolio, and help him to invest and make profit.

SUMMARY¶

In the first step, we compare 5 firms from each industry by the rate of return, which means the profit that each firms can earn. The three graphs above shows the rate of return for each company. In each graph, a higher line means the higher rate of return for this company. So, from each graph, we drop two firms that have the lowest line. For IT stock, we will drop Microsoft and HP; For retail stock, we will drop Target and Safeway; For bank stock, we will drop Citibank and Bank of America.

In the second step, we compare remained three firms in each industry by risk, which means that we choose a company that has the lowest risk. According to the F-stat, we know that the model we used has strong explaining power. We dropped the stock that has the least statistically significant of beta and proved that the intercept does differ from zero. We also used r square and p value to see how well it fit the model and whether to reject the hypothesis. Here, we use GPAM model to compare the risk. In this model, a stock fits this model most has the lowest risk, wchich means that when R square is close to 1, a stock has the lowest risk. Therefore, we compare remained three firms by R square. For IT stock, Google has the highest R square, which is 0.4738. For retail stock, Costco has the highest R square, which is 0.4422. For bank stock, JP.Morgan has the highest R square, which is 0.5733.

Therefore, we choose Google from IT industry, choose Costco from retail industry, and choose JP.Morgan from bank industry.

After choosing one stock from each industry, we want to forecast their preformance in next year. By using time series, we get weekly forecast plot for Google, Costco, and JP Morgan. From the three graphs above, the shoadow area shows the stock's preformance that we forecast in next year. From the graph, we see that the prices of all three stocks will rise in next year. This can confirm our analysis for rate of return and risk analysis is correct.

To sum up, by analysis the rate of return, risk analysis, and forecasting, we choose Google, Costco, and JP Morgan.
